creativity & curiosity.

As a product designer, I want to creatively bridge usability needs with broader business objectives and client goals, so that consumers & stakeholders alike will be fulfilled by delightful, innovative, and accessible user experiences.

I am a New York City-based designer with experience in digital publisher ad operations with a certificate in UX/UI from Columbia University Engineering and a B.S. in Media Culture & Communication from New York University, minoring in Business of Entertainment, Media, and Technology.

I have a background in display and video ad implementation & QA across desktop, mobile, tablet, and streaming channels for The Weather Channel @ IBM and NHL web & app properties. My background in advertising allows me to comprehend the technical nuances of business and monetization execution within digital media, whilst bringing a design-thinking approach to solving user-centric problems with agile and collaborative project management ability.

In my free time, you might find me creating art, playing around with code & graphic design, making playlists, attending shows & live music events, or exploring new foods in the city. ☺
